Network Installation/Configuration
Satprod provides expertise in both WAN and LAN network management and will help you configure your company's network and optimize it for speed and security.

Our services include VPN configuration for multiple sites.

Satprod can also help you make full use of your network, by helping you install Voice over IP and/or Television over IP solutions enabling you to greatly cut costs.

Finally, in conjunction with our developer's section, we can help you to make full use of the cloud both for file or website hosting as well as computing.

A growing company will quickly be confronted to device fragmentation. Satprod can help you find easy ways to deal with updating and maintaining numerous devices by creating automatic maintenance routines and setting up local update servers.

The internet of things is becoming a reality and Satprod can help you make the best of it. Using both third party devices and our own solutions, we will help you select devices adapted to your needs and use them at their full capacity.
