Satprod acquired throughout the years a catalog of skills in different areas of multimedia technologies.
Satprod's concept comes in several sections:

• Satprod Engineering
This section provides a freelance supports for other companies projects in IT development / web design and electronic solutions.
• Satprod Studio
Satprod has its own recording studio for several years, several resident engineers working on numerous multimedia projects. This studio also serves as a laboratory for the technical creative activities of the company.
• Satprod Events
Satprod provides technical supports in many events of the Belgian scene.

Satprod is there to help you maintain your project at an high level of technology without headashes.
